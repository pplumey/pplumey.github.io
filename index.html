About Me

Born on August 16, 19**, while the family was on a day trip to the State of Liberty. Grandfather dropped an icecream cone on the steps of the ferry. Upon exiting the ferry at the Statue of Liberty his mother slipped on that same icecream cone and went into labor. His birth was followed by an eight day family arguement over his name. Following this dramatic entrance into the world Peter went on to do many amazing things.
